## About My Project

EmotiGen: Crafting Emotionally Intelligent Text Through Natural Language Generation
The approach is to shorten the gap between Large language models and NLP's when it comes to being able to detect, interpret and respond in AAVE and emotion detection. In the literature review component of the paper, there has been no relevant academic paper on both emotion detection and the use of AAVE in chatbots.

Emoti-Gen, helps bring awareness to emotional health which is what makes Emoti-Gen unique from any other chat bot. By using and comparing different emotion detection models by hugging face, we are able to ensure to use the most compatible emotion detection model for our model.
